High-Five Robot

For this project I designed a functional High-Five robot that uses electronic sensors so that when you put your hand up to it the robot gives you a high five! Its equipped with LEDs, Sensors, Arduino controller, Stepper Motor resistors, etc.

Measuring and Finishing Table

For this project I made a 3D render of a Measuring and Finishing table to show my boss. They loved the idea for this, because it saved them money, time and made more quality parts from the machine. After showing them, I was able to get the necessary materials and even made the measuring apparatus that allowed the operators to measure a dimension in less than a minute when it would normally take them 5.

Isolation Part Basket

This is a project that helped save the company a lot of time, money, and potentially a customer. We were having severe isolation issues with these very small pulleys that we were making on our swiss lathes. Using the machine diagram and my knowledge of 3D printing and materials. I was able to make this parts basket that allowed complete isolation from the rest of the machine. Our clients were on the verge of leaving and I think solving this issue saved them from leaving.
